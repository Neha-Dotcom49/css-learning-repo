Top 7 Places To Visit MadhyaPradesh

Madhya Pradesh, state of India. As its name implies—madhya means “central” and pradesh means “region” or “state”—it is situated in the heart of the country.

Khajuraho

A small town located in the Bundelkhand region, Khajuraho is a brilliant example of Indian architecture

Mandu Full Day Tour From Indore

Experiencing the medieval Mughal highlights of Mandu—now known as Mandav—can be tricky by cab or bus from Indore.

Bhedaghat

Bhedaghat is known for the Dhuandar Falls (Dhuan means smoke and Dhar means flow), a huge cascade of water that falls from a height of 98 feet
